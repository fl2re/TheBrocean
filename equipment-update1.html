New Lights!

by Alex D/February 17, 2016

The Ecotech Radions finally came in. I bought them used but I've tested them and they work perfectly. Unfortunately I had nothing to hang them with. You can buy arms that attach to the tank but they're $100 a pop and there's no way I'm paying that much for bent metal. So this past weekend my Dad taught me how to weld and we got to work!

Welding and grinding down the metal took about 6 hours but the finished product looks great. All that needs to be done now is to sand down the metal and give it a matte black spraypaint finish.
