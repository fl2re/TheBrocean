About Author

I will update this when I feel like talking about myself but for now...

I am a 24 year old mobile app developer and graphic designer. I've been interested in fish since I was 6 years old and have periodically owned tanks throughout my life. My first reef tank started in August 2015 but I had been researching everyday since May 2015. I know a lot but there's so much more I can learn from the community and the web.

I have a good/bad habit of finding something interesting, binge-learning everything I can get my hands on, and then losing interest once I've consumed everything I can find. Fortunately, reefing has held my interest longer than anything else and I've still got so much more to learn. It will be a great experience seeing everything grow.

Purpose of this blog

Note: This is going to be a goofy blog. I take very few things seriously and like to mess around.

I have created this blog to track the progress of my new 180g tank as well as learning some web programming along the way. By the end of this, I would like to see a start to finish timeline of everything I have done to this tank. All blog posts will contain pictures and I will update the photo gallery with as many non-repetitive images as possible.
